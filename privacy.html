Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ToolsNova ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://toolsnova.github.io and use our tools.

We believe in complete transparency. We do not sell your data, and we only collect what's necessary to provide you with the best experience.

No Data Selling

We never sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

Secure Authentication

Your password is encrypted by Firebase - we never see it.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you sign up for an account, we collect:

  • Email address: Used for login and account management
  • Encrypted password: Securely stored by Firebase Authentication
  • Account creation date: For account management purposes

2.2 Guest Usage Data

For users who don't sign up, we store locally in your browser:

  • Tool usage count: Limited to 3 uses (stored in localStorage)
  • Dark mode preference: Your theme preference
This data never leaves your browser and is cleared when you clear your browser cache.

2.3 Usage Analytics

We use basic analytics to improve our tools:

  • Which tools are most popular (aggregated data only)
  • General geographic location (country level)
  • Browser type and device information

This data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to individual users.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for these purposes only:

  • To provide our tools: Making sure you can use all features
  • To manage your account: Login, authentication, and account settings
  • To improve user experience: Understanding which tools to enhance
  • To enforce guest limits: 3 free uses for non-logged-in users

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase for user authentication. Firebase stores your email and encrypted password. View Firebase Privacy Policy →

4.2 Groq AI

Our AI Assistant uses Groq's API. When you use the AI chat, your messages are sent to Groq for processing. View Groq Privacy Policy →

4.3 Font Awesome & Google Fonts

We use these for icons and typography. They may collect usage data as described in their policies.

4.4 GitHub Pages

Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs. View GitHub Privacy Statement →

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage for:

  • Authentication tokens: Keeping you logged in
  • Theme preference: Remembering your dark/light mode choice
  • Guest usage counter: Tracking free tool usage

We do not use tracking cookies or cross-site tracking.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to deletion: Request account deletion
  • Right to correction: Fix inaccurate information
  • Right to opt-out: Decline data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our third-party providers operate. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

Have Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

GitHub: @toolsnova

We typically respond within 48 hours.
