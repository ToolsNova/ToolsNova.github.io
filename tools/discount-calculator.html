Frequently Asked Questions

How to calculate discount percentage off online for free?

Simply enter the original price and the discount percentage. Our calculator instantly shows you the final price and how much you save. It's completely free and works with 9+ currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, INR, and more!

What is the formula to calculate discount price?

The discount calculation formula is: Savings = Original Price × (Discount % ÷ 100). Final Price = Original Price - Savings. For example, $100 at 20% off = $100 × 0.20 = $20 savings, final price = $80.

How to calculate original price after discount?

To find the original price when you know the discounted price and discount percentage, use: Original Price = Discounted Price ÷ (1 - Discount % ÷ 100). Our calculator focuses on discount calculation, but you can reverse the formula manually.

What currencies are supported?

We support 9+ currencies: USD ($), EUR (€), GBP (£), JPY (¥), INR (₹), CAD (CA$), AUD (A$), CHF, and CNY (CN¥). More currencies coming soon!

How to calculate 10%, 15%, 20%, or 25% off?

Simply enter the original price and the discount percentage (10, 15, 20, 25, etc.). For example, $100 at 20% off = $20 saved, final price $80. The calculator works for any percentage from 0-100%.

Can I use this for VAT or tax calculations?

While this tool is designed for discounts, you can use it for tax calculations by entering the tax percentage as the "discount" percentage. For VAT, enter original price + tax%. We also have dedicated tax calculators coming soon!

Is this discount calculator free to use?

Yes, completely free! Guest users get 3 free calculations. For unlimited access, sign up for free - no credit card required.

How to calculate multiple discounts or buy-one-get-one deals?

For multiple discounts, apply them sequentially. For BOGO deals, calculate based on the actual price paid. For example, buy one get one 50% off means you pay full price for one and half for the second - our calculator can help with each transaction individually.
