Frequently Asked Questions

How to edit images online for free?

Simply upload your image, then use the editing tools: crop, resize, rotate, adjust brightness/contrast/saturation, apply filters, or use auto-enhance. All edits happen in your browser - no upload required!

Does this image editor work offline?

Yes! Your images never leave your device. All editing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. This means 100% privacy, no upload delays, and you can use it offline after the page loads.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, WebP, and GIF formats. Maximum file size is 50MB. You can export your edited image as JPG, PNG, or WebP with adjustable quality settings.

How to crop an image accurately?

Click "Start Cropping", then drag the crop handles to select the area you want to keep. Click "Apply" to finalize the crop, or "Cancel" to discard changes. The crop tool supports free-form cropping.

Can I resize while maintaining aspect ratio?

Yes! Check the "Maintain aspect ratio" checkbox when resizing. This ensures your image proportions stay correct - when you change width, height updates automatically and vice versa.

What filters are available?

Available filters: Normal, Grayscale, Sepia, Invert, Vintage, Cool, Warm, and Auto Enhance. Auto Enhance automatically optimizes brightness, contrast, and saturation for the best result.

Is this image editor free to use?

Yes, completely free! Guest users get 3 free edits. For unlimited access, sign up for free - no credit card required.

Can I undo changes if I make a mistake?

Yes! Use the Undo/Redo buttons in the History section to revert or reapply changes. You can also click "Reset All Changes" to start over with the original image.
