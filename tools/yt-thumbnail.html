Frequently Asked Questions

How to download YouTube thumbnails in HD for free?

Simply paste your YouTube video URL into the input field and click "Get Thumbnails". You'll see all available thumbnail sizes (from 120x90 to 1280x720 HD). Choose the quality you want and click download. No registration required!

What thumbnail qualities are available?

Multiple qualities: Default (120x90px), Medium (320x180px), High (480x360px), Standard (640x480px), and Max Resolution (1280x720px HD). For some popular videos, 4K thumbnails (3840x2160px) may also be available.

How to get the highest quality YouTube thumbnail?

Our tool automatically fetches the best available quality (maxresdefault). For most videos, this is 1280x720px HD. Some popular videos have 4K thumbnails available. The download button shows the resolution before downloading.

What YouTube URL formats are supported?

All YouTube URL formats: standard (youtube.com/watch?v=...), shortened (youtu.be/...), embed links (youtube.com/embed/...), and YouTube Shorts (youtube.com/shorts/...). Also supports URLs with timestamps and playlist parameters.

Can I download thumbnails from private or age-restricted videos?

Private, unlisted, and age-restricted videos may not be accessible. Our tool only works with public videos where thumbnails are hosted on YouTube's public CDN. Most public videos work perfectly.

What is the file format of downloaded thumbnails?

Thumbnails are downloaded in JPG/JPEG format. This is the native format YouTube uses for thumbnails, offering good quality with reasonable file size. Downloads are direct from YouTube's servers.

Is this YouTube thumbnail downloader free?

Yes, completely free! Guest users get 3 free downloads. For unlimited access, sign up for free - no credit card required.

Why can't I find thumbnails for some videos?

Possible reasons: Invalid URL format, video is private/unlisted, video was removed, or the thumbnail hasn't been generated yet. Double-check the URL and try again. Most public videos work fine.
