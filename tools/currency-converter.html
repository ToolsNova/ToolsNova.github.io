Frequently Asked Questions

How to convert currency online for free?

Simply enter the amount, select your source currency (From) and target currency (To), then click "Convert". Our tool uses real-time exchange rates from reliable APIs to give you accurate conversions instantly. No registration required!

What's the difference between Basic and Advanced mode?

Basic mode covers 30+ major currencies from the European Central Bank - perfect for everyday conversions. Advanced mode covers 150+ currencies including rare and exotic ones, powered by FreeCurrencyAPI with 5,000 monthly requests.

How often are exchange rates updated?

Both converters update their exchange rates daily. Basic mode uses ECB data (daily updates), while Advanced mode also updates daily. Last update time is displayed with each conversion result.

What currencies are available in Basic mode?

Basic mode includes 30+ major currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, AUD, CAD, CHF, CNY, INR, BRL, RUB, KRW, IDR, TRY, SAR, AED, ZAR, MXN, SGD, NZD, HKD, NOK, SEK, DKK, PLN, CZK, HUF, ILS, THB, MYR, PHP, and more.

Is this currency converter accurate for travel?

Yes! Our converter uses official exchange rates from reliable sources. Perfect for travel planning, online shopping, or business calculations. Note that actual exchange rates from banks may include additional fees.

How to convert USD to INR or EUR to USD?

Simply select USD as "From" and INR as "To" (or EUR to USD), enter the amount, and click Convert. You'll get the real-time exchange rate and converted amount instantly. Popular pairs like USD to INR, USD to EUR are pre-loaded.

Is this currency converter free to use?

Yes, completely free! Guest users get 3 free conversions. For unlimited access, sign up for free - no credit card required.

Can I convert between exotic currencies like THB to IDR?

Yes! Use the Advanced mode which supports 150+ currencies including Thai Baht (THB), Indonesian Rupiah (IDR), Vietnamese Dong (VND), and many more exotic and rare currencies from around the world.
