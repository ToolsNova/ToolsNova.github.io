Frequently Asked Questions

How to create a QR code for free?

Simply enter your URL or text, choose the size and error correction level, then click "Generate QR Code". Your QR code will be created instantly. Download it as PNG or copy it to your clipboard. No registration required!

What can I use QR codes for?

QR codes can store website URLs, email addresses, WiFi credentials, phone numbers, locations, contact information, app download links, payment links, social media profiles, and any text you want to share quickly.

What is error correction in QR codes?

Error correction allows QR codes to remain readable even if partially damaged or covered. Options: L (7% recovery), M (15% recovery), Q (25% recovery), H (30% recovery). Higher levels make QR codes slightly larger but more durable.

What's the ideal QR code size for printing?

For business cards: 1-2 cm (200-300px). For posters/flyers: 3-5 cm (400-500px). For outdoor signage: 10-15 cm or larger. The scanning distance should be about 10x the QR code width. Higher error correction (Q or H) is recommended for printed QR codes.

Can I customize QR code colors?

Currently, we support standard black and white QR codes for maximum compatibility with all scanners. Colored QR codes sometimes have scanning issues with older devices. We recommend black on white for best results.

How to make a QR code for WiFi?

Enter this format in the content field: WIFI:S:NetworkName;T:WPA;P:Password;; Replace NetworkName with your SSID, WPA with security type (WPA/WEP/nopass), and Password with your WiFi password. Scan to connect instantly!

Does this QR code generator track my data?
